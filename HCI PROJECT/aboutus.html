DanBam

Our goal was simple when we decided to create this restaurant 5 years ago. It is to introduce and catch the attention of young generations on traditional korean cuisine. We pack our foods with modern packaging and decorate our restaurant with neon signs to grab the attention of the young ones. Our foods are also cooked by the best chef in South Korea and we make sure to provide our customers with the best service and high quality food ingredients.

Our Story

  • 2016

    Danbam main branch was created in Seoul

  • 2017

    Danbam’s first branch in Busan was created

  • 2018

    We opened our rooftops for public dining and created another branch in Daegu

  • 2019

    Our branch in Incheon, near Incheon Airport was created

  • 2020

    Won the World Culinary Awards for South Korea’s Best Restaurant 2020
